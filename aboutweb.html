ABMConnectEd, your go-to online platform for students pursuing the Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) track in Senior High School. Our website is dedicated to providing comprehensive educational resources, support, and a sense of community for ABM students like you.

OUR MISSION

ABMConnectEd, our mission is to empower and assist ABM students in their academic journey by offering a wide range of valuable resources and tools. We aim to bridge the gap between students and educational content, fostering a positive and enriching learning experience.

Our website offers a range of valuable features and services to ensure a seamless and enriching learning experience:

Curriculum Links

Access a curated collection of links to essential Senior High School (SHS) ABM subjects, including core, contextualized, and specialized courses.

Student Showcase

Discover the outstanding achievements and projects of ABM students, showcasing their talent and dedication.

Forums and Testimonials

Engage in interactive forums to discuss academic topics, share knowledge, and seek guidance from fellow ABM students. Read inspiring testimonials from successful ABM graduates.

Join the Community

Become part of a vibrant ABM community, fostering connections, peer support, and networking opportunities.

About the Developers

ABMConnectEd is proudly brought to you by a team of dedicated developers who share a passion for education and technology. We are scholars of Passerelles numériques, an organization committed to providing quality education and training in the field of technology to underserved youth.


As scholars of Passerelles numériques, we are currently pursuing a Certificate I in Computer Technology with a major in Software Development. Our journey with Passerelles numériques has equipped us with the skills and knowledge to create a platform like ABMConnectEd, designed to empower and support students in the ABM strand.


Our team is determined to make ABMConnectEd a valuable resource for every ABM student. We believe that education is a powerful tool that can transform lives and open doors to countless opportunities.


Through our combined efforts, we have curated educational content, designed interactive features, and built a community-driven space that encourages collaboration and learning.
